About Us


The International Foundation of Senegalese Twins and Their Parents, a nonprofit organization was begun in 2006 in Senegal and has been the salvation of hundreds of children there. The International Foundation of Senegalese Twins and Their Parents, Inc. is a nonsectarian organization funded by private donations. Its primary founder, Khady MBacke, has worked successfully with Senegalese mothers and children for more than 11 years. The International Foundation of Senegalese Twins and Their Parents, Inc., was later incorporated in the United States and received its 501(c)(3) in June 2016. The philosophy of the International Foundation of Senegalese Twins and Their Parents, Inc., is that we must expose youth to positive influences within a framework of activities and experiences that help them flourish and reach their full potential. This non-profit organization was founded in 2004 for the specific purpose of promoting the special aspects of child development for multiple birth children. They have tons of information pertaining to multiple birth, parenting, as well as research and conventions.
